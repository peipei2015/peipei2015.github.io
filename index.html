About Me

I am currently an Associate Researcher at the School of Information Science and Technology, USTC. I completed my postdoctoral training (2023.07–2025.06) at USTC under Prof. Xun Yang. I received my B.S. and Ph.D. from Hefei University of Technology, advised by Prof. Meng Wang and Prof. Dan Guo.

My research centers on multimodal intelligence, including cross-modal retrieval, visual captioning, multimodal emotion reasoning. My long-term goal is to build empathetic, human-centric AI systems. Welcome academic cooperation!
